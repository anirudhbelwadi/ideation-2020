What is ideation?

About Ideation

Ideation, provides a common platform for great thinkers and enthusiasts to come up with technically productive and creative ideas. It is a medium through which students can brainstorm solutions for real-life problem statements. Our aim is to give back to the society what is taught to us, and inculcate technology in real-life problem solutions for the betterment of the society. The solutions can be taken up as a firsthand project in the future. The participants will be given a problem statement which will be relevant to the society and they will be given time to bridge the gaps that our NGO partners face to implement such ideas. At the end of the session the participants have to present their ideas and the most appropriate, optimal, practical and unique idea wins the competition.The winners will be given cash prizes and goodies. The participants can be from various technical backgrounds.

What are the instructions?

Rules

  • All participants must strictly follow the schedule attached, to avoid delays and inconveniences.
  • Venue coordinators have been assigned at each venue and would be directing you to the allotted areas for refreshments and presentations. Approach them in case of any distress or issues.
  • Kindly do not leave the assigned venue and loiter within the campus. If you need anything, please approach the venue coordinators.
  • There would be a short refreshment break at 1:00 PM for 30 mins. If a team finishes early, they can return to the assigned venue and resume with their planning and preparation
  • Participants must be ready with their presentations by 2:30 PM i.e. before the first round of judging begins.
  • Results for the first round would be declared by 3:30 PM in the GST Auditorium. Once done with their presentation, all participants must assemble in the said venue.
  • Since the event is being conducted during the college’s technical festival, participants can visit the various events and food stalls in the allotted refreshments break.
  • All participants are requested to stay within the college premises till the end of the program. Participation certificates would be distributed only during the valedictory ceremony.
  • Participants would be held liable for any damage to college property during the event. In such cases, Ideation ‘19 organizing committee is not responsible for the damage caused.
